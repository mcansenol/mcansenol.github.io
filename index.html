Welcome to IcesCrafts! We are so grateful that you found our store. My name is Can, I am a mechanical engineer who has been producing pure titanium material for many years.

  • Handcrafted in Istanbul by experienced mechanical engineer
  • Premium titanium material - lightweight and hypoallergenic
  • Free worldwide shipping with custom engraving service

I have designed and manufactured many products related to titanium in the healthcare sector. As an engineer, the unique durability and lightness provided by titanium has always fed my desire to offer elegant and long-lasting designs to my customers.
